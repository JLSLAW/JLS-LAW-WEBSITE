About JLS LAW

JLS LAW was founded in December 2023 with a mission to make quality legal education affordable and accessible to all aspirants. What began as a coaching platform for CLAT, AILET, LSAT—India, CUET UG, CUET PG, MHCET LAW, PUCET, SLS SET, and more has now grown into a comprehensive legal education ecosystem.

Recorded sessions and 24/7 access.

Growth & Expansion

In 2025, JLS LAW received MSME Certification from the Government of India. The platform expanded into internships, competitions, publications, and workshops—providing practical exposure beyond entrance exams.

Our Mission

To empower future legal professionals with knowledge, skills, and confidence to crack exams and succeed in the legal field.
